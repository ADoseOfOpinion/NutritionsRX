About Me

Hello, I am M.S. Yan Lin, RDN, LDN, currently licensed to serve PA & NJ residents.

I’m a clinical dietitian with a passion for empowering individuals to lead healthier lives through better nutrition. With many years of clinical experience in hospital settings and senior retirement centers, I've had the privilege of witnessing firsthand the transformative power of nutrition on health and well-being.

My Background

I obtained my Bachelor's degree in dietetics from Miami University and a Master's degree in nutrition education and counseling from Immaculata University. I've been dedicated to continuing my passion for nutrition education and practice. Through ongoing learning and professional development, I strive to stay current with the latest research and practices in the field of nutrition.

Our Mission

At NutritionsRX, our mission is to empower individuals to lead healthier lives through personalized nutrition interventions. Whether you're managing weight or dealing with chronic conditions such as diabetes, heart disease, cancer, gout, digestive diseases, or chronic kidney disease, we're here to provide expert support tailored to you. I believe that by adhering to current, scientifically approved nutrition practices and making small, consistent progress, individuals can make a remarkable impact on their overall wellness and experience a sense of accomplishment.

Why Nutrition Matters

Throughout my career, I've seen how simple dietary changes can lead to profound improvements in health outcomes. Whether it's managing chronic conditions such as diabetes or heart disease, or simply striving for a healthier lifestyle, nutrition plays a critical role in achieving optimal well-being.

My Approach

I take a practical and compassionate approach to nutrition counseling, focusing on realistic strategies that fit into each individual's unique lifestyle and preferences. By providing education, guidance, and ongoing support, I aim to empower my clients to make sustainable changes that lead to long-term health benefits.

Let's Work Together

Whether you're looking to manage a specific health condition, improve your overall well-being, or simply enhance your nutrition knowledge, I'm here to help. Let's embark on this journey together and unlock the full potential of your health through better nutrition.
